Quokkas

The quokka is a small macropod about the size of a domestic cat. It is the only member of the genus Setonix. Like other marsupials in the macropod family (such as kangaroos and wallabies), the quokka is herbivorous and mainly nocturnal

The Tasmanian devil is in a group of animals on the verge of extinction that need to be preserved. The Tasmanian devil is a marsupial. These non-animals will live in the mother's pouch from birth until the fourth month. Possessing a distinctive black coat and symbolic size about the size of a small puppy, the Tasmanian Devil is a very lazy animal. A nocturnal animal, the Tasmanian Devil can only be found after the sun has completely set. Tasmanian devils are carnivorous, and they catch their prey with their fangs and claws.
